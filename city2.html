About Los Angeles

Los Angeles, known for its entertainment industry, diverse culture, and beautiful landscapes, is one of the most iconic cities in the world.

Population

Los Angeles has a population of approximately 3.8 million people.

Year Incorporated

Los Angeles was incorporated on April 4th, 1850, making it one of the major cities established in California's early statehood.

Region

Los Angeles is situated in Southern California, along the Pacific coast, known for its Mediterranean climate and beautiful beaches.

Classifications

Los Angeles is classified as an urban city, renowned for its sprawling metropolitan area, vibrant arts scene, and major cultural institutions.

Average Income Level

The median household income in Los Angeles is about $63,142, which is lower than the California state median income.

Top Attractions

  1. Hollywood Walk of Fame
    • The Hollywood Walk of Fame honors celebrities with stars embedded in the sidewalks along Hollywood Boulevard and Vine Street.
  2. Griffith Observatory
    • The Griffith Observatory offers stunning views of Los Angeles and the Hollywood Sign, along with exhibits and a planetarium.
  3. Griffith Park
    • Griffith Park is one of the largest urban parks in North America, featuring hiking trails, picnic areas, and the Los Angeles Zoo.
  4. Universal Studios Hollywood
    • A film studio and theme park, Universal Studios Hollywood offers thrilling rides, shows, and a behind-the-scenes look at movie-making magic.
  5. Santa Monica Pier
    • The Santa Monica Pier is a historic landmark featuring an amusement park, aquarium, family-friendly resturants, and stunning ocean views. It's a perfect spot for a day out with family and friends.
